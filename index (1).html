Danny's Donuts has been serving the Flowery Branch community with freshly made donuts, breakfast sandwiches, and hot coffee every single morning. We open at 5AM so you never have to start your day without something great.

From classic glazed to fancy fritters, kolache to breakfast burritos — everything is made with care and served with a smile. We are your neighborhood donut shop, plain and simple.
